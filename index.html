Threshing Rice

Threshing is the process of separating the rice grains from the stalks. This can be done manually by beating the stalks against a hard surface or using a machine called a thresher.

Making Tempe

Mixing soybean with a tempeh starter, which is a mixture of live mold spores (usually Rhizopus oligosporus) and a grain such as rice or barley. The starter begins the fermentation process.
